    Who am I

    My name is Samuel Pattinson, I'm from Wellington NZ, and I'm a programmer. I hraduated from the University of Canterbury in 2021 in Civil Engineering, which is where I discovered my passion in programming. Ever since then, I've been self--studying various topics within Computer Science as i work towards starting my Masters in Computer Science at Victoria University in 2025.

    My passions in this field include various aerospace topics, Artificial Intelligence, and creating programs that help shape the world we live. I'm a firm believer that our greatest successes in life are born from our collection of failures, as it's the pursuit of knowledge that drives us to become great. I myself have failed many times, only to rise up hungrier than before. You'll see this in action throughout my portfolio of projects!

    What's my background

    During my studies in Civil Engineering, I came across various ways to program in order to solve problems. This mainly ranged from Python, Matlab, and various structural engineering software packages. I found that I was more interested in how these things work, and dramed of the many things I could create with them.

    I worked in the Civil Engineering industry for a few years before I ultimately decided that programming is the life-long career I want for myself, where I'm able to express myself creatively. If my studies taught me anything, it's how to think like an engineer, which I believe is one of my strong suits when it comes to problem solving.

    What drives me

    I get alot of inspiration for my topics of interest from the aerospace idnustry, where i'm fascinated by the idea of space travel and discovering what's out there. When i'm feeling burnt out, this idea is exactly what keeps me going. I'm an innovator at heart with a skillset I believe can help shape the world.
