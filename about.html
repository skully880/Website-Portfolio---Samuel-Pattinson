Who am I

My name is Samuel Pattinson, I'm from Wellington NZ, and I'm a Computer Programmer and Engineer. I studied Engineering at the University of Canterbury from 2017 to 2021, where I discovered my passion in programming. I'm currently self-studying various topics within Computer Science as I work towards starting my Masters in Artificial Inteligence at Victoria University, early in 2025.

I love learning new things every day as my greatest happiness in life is the pursuit for knowledge. I actively try to implement things as I learn them, regardless of failures, as this is where I find my greatest successes are created. You'll find that my portfolio of projects is a culmination of the knowledge and practise I have done, ever-expanding, as I progress throughout my career! I hope to one day become an expert in the tech and space industry, particularly in programming and AI.

What drives me

I love all things programming! From learning new languages, to working on exciting projects and diving into the realm of Artificial Intelligence, I simply can't get enough of it. I'm an explorer at heart, and as I explore different languages, I'm continuing to expand my horizon of ideas. The opportunities with programming are endless, and the ideas you can have are limitless.

This concept of endless opportunity is exactly why I love this field, never forgetting that we are standing on the shoulders of all who came before us on this journey. Check out my portfolio of projects to see what I've been working on, and what I've got planned for the future!
